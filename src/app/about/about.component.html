ABOUT

Started sports at 5 and yoga in 2007 at the age of 13.In Feb 2012 I got injured losing the ability to walk.
I resumed my yoga ,I noticed I was getting better, with time I was able to walk and eventually even run.
As time went, I resumed sports and became a coach.

It was yoga that inspired me and helped me when doctors said I wouldn't walk again. Following these circumstances, I decided to teach what I learned to help others heal.
throughout my journey. I approach asanas in a holistic way combining Strength, balance and flexibility.
